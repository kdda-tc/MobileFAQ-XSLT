What is Mobile Print?

Mobile Print is a convenient app for iOS and Android that lets you use your mobile device to connect wirelessly to compatible printing devices and MFPs on your local area network. When connected, you can do the following:

  • Print photos and documents stored on your mobile device.
  • Scan documents on a compatible MFP and save them to your mobile device.
  • Send documents by e-mail.
  • Print webpages accessed through the app's built-in web browser.

Where can I download Mobile Print?

The app is available for download from the iTunes App Store (for iOS devices) and Google Play (for Android devices).

How much does Mobile Print cost?

The app is free.
